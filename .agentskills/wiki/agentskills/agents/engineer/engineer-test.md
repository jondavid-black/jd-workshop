# engineer-test

The **`engineer-test`** is a specialized engineering subagent.

## 1. Capabilities
- Designs unit/integration tests and maps requirements to scenario checks.
- Generates Gherkin feature files and verifies trace coverage in `traceability-report.md`.
- Operates primarily during Phase 4 (Implementation).
