# Reference Sandbox

The **Reference Sandbox** is located inside `.agentskills/references/`.

## 1. Capabilities
- Keeps downloaded external files (such as PDF papers or raw database scripts) safely sandboxed.
- Added to `.gitignore` to prevent committing heavy binaries or temporary PDFs to the repository.
- Used exclusively by the **Researcher Agent** to inspect and explore content.
