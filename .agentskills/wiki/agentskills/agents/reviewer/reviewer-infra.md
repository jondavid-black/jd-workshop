# reviewer-infra

The **`reviewer-infra`** is a specialized read-only auditing subagent.

## 1. Capabilities
- Audits declarative Kubernetes manifests, Ansible roles, and docker orchestration.
- Scans files for exposed passwords, keys, or non-sandboxed setups.
