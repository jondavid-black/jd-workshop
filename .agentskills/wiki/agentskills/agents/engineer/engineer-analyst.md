# engineer-analyst

The **`engineer-analyst`** is a specialized engineering subagent.

## 1. Capabilities
- Clarifies ambiguous requirements, stress-tests user plans, and maps concepts to standard vocabulary.
- Generates and writes standardized Product Requirements Documents (PRDs).
- Operates primarily during Phase 1 (Domain Refinement).
