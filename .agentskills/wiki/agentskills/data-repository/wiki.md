# OKF Wiki Root (wiki/)

The **`wiki/`** folder is located under `.agentskills/wiki/` and serves as our global wiki knowledge base root.

## 1. Capabilities
- Houses one or more self-contained, portable **Knowledge Bundles** (the first subdirectory must always be the bundle name: e.g., `.agentskills/wiki/agentskills/`).
- Root-level indices recursively route queries down into individual bundles.
